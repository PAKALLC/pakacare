Home Care Services

Care in the Comfort of Your Own Home

Our Home Care Services are designed for individuals who wish to remain in their own home while receiving support. Under this model, a trained caregiver travels to the client’s residence to provide non-medical assistance based on an individualized care plan.

This service is ideal for seniors who need help with daily tasks, adults recovering from hospitalization, or individuals with disabilities who require regular supervision and support.

Personal Care Assistance

We assist clients with essential daily activities, including:

  • Bathing and personal hygiene
  • Dressing and grooming
  • Toileting and incontinence care
  • Mobility and transfer assistance
  • Ambulation support and fall prevention
  • Feeding assistance
  • Medication reminders (non-clinical)
Homemaker Services

We help maintain a safe and comfortable home environment:

  • Housekeeping
  • Laundry and linen changes
  • Meal preparation (including special dietary needs)
  • Grocery shopping and errands
  • Prescription pickup
  • Dishwashing and kitchen cleaning
Companion & Support Services

We provide emotional support and engagement to enhance quality of life:

  • Conversation and companionship
  • Recreational activities
  • Assistance with hobbies
  • Accompaniment to medical appointments
  • Community outings
  • Non-medical transportation assistance
Flexible Care Options
  • Hourly care (Minimum 4hours per visit)
  • Overnight support
  • Live-in care
  • 24-hour service availability
  • Customized care plans
  • Ongoing supervision and quality monitoring by the agency

We proudly serve Medicaid (CFI/HCBC) participants as well as private pay clients.
