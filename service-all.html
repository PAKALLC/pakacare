PAKA LLC Services

At PAKA LLC, we provide compassionate, non-medical care services across New Hampshire. We support elderly individuals and adults with disabilities who need assistance with daily living, supervision, and companionship in a safe and respectful environment.

We offer two primary service models so families can choose the level of support that best meets their needs:

  1. Home Care Services – Care provided in the client’s own home
  2. Adult Family Care Residence – AFCR – 24/7 care in a certified caregiver’s home

Both options focus on dignity, safety, independence, and personalized care.
