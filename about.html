Our Vision

At PAKA LLC, our vision is to create a safe and caring environment where elderly individuals and adults with disabilities feel respected, valued, and supported every day. We strive to provide care that feels like family, not a facility. Our goal is to bring peace of mind to families across New Hampshire.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide compassionate, reliable, and personalized non-medical care services that protect dignity and promote independence. Through Passion, Accountability, Kindness & Action (PAKA), we are committed to delivering care with honesty, respect, and responsibility.

Why Choose PAKA LLC

  • Non-medical care provider serving New Hampshire
  • Compassionate and carefully screened caregivers
  • Personalized care plans based on individual needs
  • Safe home care and shared living options
  • Flexible service hours (hourly, overnight, live-in and 24/7)
  • Medicaid and private pay options available
  • Strong commitment to Passion, Accountability, Kindness & Action
