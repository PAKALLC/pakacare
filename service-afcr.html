AFCR / Shared Living

24/7 Care in a Home-Like Environment

Our Adult Family Care Residence (AFCR) model, also known as Shared Living, provides around-the-clock support in a certified caregiver’s home. This option is ideal for individuals who can no longer live safely alone and require continuous supervision and daily assistance.

Unlike institutional settings, AFCR offers a family-style environment where participants live in a private residence and receive personalized support.

What AFCR Includes
24/7 Supervision and Support
  • Continuous oversight
  • Structured daily routine
  • Safe and secure living environment
  • Assistance with daily living activities
Personal Care Services
  • Bathing and grooming
  • Dressing
  • Toileting support
  • Feeding assistance
  • Mobility support
  • Medication reminders per care plan
Meals and Nutrition
  • Three daily home-cooked meals
  • Snacks
  • Special diet accommodations when required
Community Integration
  • Social and recreational activities
  • Community outings
  • Religious or cultural participation (if desired)
  • Family involvement and communication
Health Coordination
  • Appointment scheduling
  • Transportation to appointments
  • Communication with case managers
  • Ongoing coordination with family members
Comfortable Living Environment
  • Furnished bedroom
  • Clean and well-maintained home
  • Laundry services
  • Utilities included

Our AFCR homes are available to Medicaid participants as well as private pay individuals seeking a safe, family-style living environment.
